A. REARLY-BIRD REGISTRATION FEES

Category Fees
Faculty/Academician Participants INR 4500
Research Scholars/Students INR 4000
Industry Participants INR 5500
Foreign Participants USD 200

Note:

For students, only one paper per registration is acceptable. This fee includes 1 tutorial access for student registrant.

Author Registration

  • Each paper must be covered by a full IEEE Member or Non-Member (as the case may be) registration fee.
  • Each full registration fee can cover presentation of up to 2 papers.
  • Registration fee is inclusive of applicable GST.

Register here

Registration fee includes

  • Access to all technical sessions
  • Tea/Coffee breaks, lunches during the conference
  • Welcome reception
  • Conference banquet
  • Registration kit

However, registrations DOES NOT include the following:

  • Hotel stay during the conference
  • Access to Tutorial(s) for which separate registration is required

* Accompanying guest/spouse, if attending, must register under 'Non-authors' category.

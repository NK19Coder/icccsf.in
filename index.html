About

Vivekananda Institute of Professional Studies (VIPS) is a reputed institute that is situated in Pitampura, New Delhi, India. The Institute is affiliated with Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU). The institute was established in 1997 by Dr. S. C. Vats, who is an educationalist and a great philanthropist. VIPS is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). It has been accredited by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) with an A grade.

The institute is centrally air-conditioned and provides all the state-of-art facilities to students that can help them in excelling in their field. Keeping in mind the latest trends & technology in education, VIPS-TC has established college of Engineering. The institute offers state-of-art facilities for students to study and excel in these programs. VIPS-TC College of Engineering is built on the vision and teachings of Swami Vivekananda, to sharpen intellect, chisel character and harness possibilities of tomorrow through a rigorous curriculum, innovative pedagogy and holistic growth approach.
